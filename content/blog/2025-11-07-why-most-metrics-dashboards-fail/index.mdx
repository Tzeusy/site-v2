## Dashboards Should Answer Decisions

A dashboard is useful only when it drives a concrete action. If a graph looks
impressive but does not change what you do, it is decoration.

## Watch for Metric Theater

Metric theater appears when teams collect everything and prioritize nothing.
Choose a small set of signals tied to user outcomes and error budgets.

## Build for Triage Speed

During incidents, the right panel is the one that reduces next-step ambiguity.
That usually means fewer charts, tighter labels, and explicit thresholds.
